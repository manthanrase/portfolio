CONTEXT

Designing safer emotional expression for everyone.

Students and young professionals often struggle with loneliness and emotional distress but avoid seeking help due to stigma, fear of judgment, or lack of privacy. Existing social and mental health apps felt too public, too impersonal, or too formal. I saw an opportunity to design a space that respects emotional vulnerability while enabling meaningful, anonymous connection.

FIRST PRINCIPLES

Breaking down the problem from the ground up.

Applying first principles thinking helped us move beyond assumptions and design Moodly to address real, fundamental user needs:
